# Men Gallant FC · Justaino PS Tool - User Guide

Add PlayStyles and PlayStyle+ to your EA FC 26 club players **in bulk**, in seconds -
without clicking through the game's menus one evolution at a time.

This is a friendly guide to *using* the tool. (If you're maintaining the code, see
`RUNBOOK.md` instead.)

---

## What it does

Pick a club player, tick the PlayStyles you want, hit **Apply** - the tool adds them
all for you, one after another, and shows you exactly what landed. It works entirely
inside the EA FC web app using your own logged-in session, so there's nothing to sign
into and nothing leaves your browser.

---

## Getting started

1. **Save the bookmarklet.** Add the one line from `bookmarklet.txt` as a browser
   bookmark (on the address bar / bookmarks bar).
2. **Open the EA FC 26 Web App** and let it load.
3. **Click the bookmark.** The panel appears - bottom-right on a computer, or as a
   sheet from the bottom on a phone.

That's it. Click it again any time to refresh it.

---

## The good stuff (features)

**⚽ Your whole club, not just your squad.**
It loads *every* player you own - hundreds of cards - so you can evolve anyone, not
only your starting XI.

**🔎 Instant search.**
Start typing a name to filter the list. Players are sorted by rating, best first.

**✨ Smart Suggestions.**
Not sure which PlayStyles suit a player? Pick a **position** and a **role**
(e.g. RW/LW → Inside Forward) and hit **✨ Suggest** - it pre-ticks a proven set,
with the three best as PlayStyle+.

**👀 See PlayStyle+ at a glance.**
Each player in the list shows the **PlayStyle+ icons they already have**, in gold,
right next to their name - so you can spot what a card's got without opening it.

**🎯 A rich preview of any player.**
Select someone and you'll see their rating, rarity, positions, and two neat **slot
meters**: 3 for PlayStyle+ (gold) and 8 for Basic (emerald), filling up as slots get
used. Their current PlayStyles appear as tidy chips underneath.

**🟢 "Only evo-eligible" filter that learns.**
Only certain special cards can receive PlayStyles. Flip on **Only evo-eligible** and
the list hides the cards that can't. Best part: **it learns automatically** - every
time an apply succeeds, that card type is remembered as eligible, so the filter gets
smarter the more you use it. You can also mark/un-mark any card by hand.

**⚡ Bulk apply with a satisfying finish.**
Tick as many PlayStyles as you like (up to the caps: 3 PS+, 8 Basic). Hit **Apply**
and watch each one **spin, then stamp a green tick** as it lands. At the end you get a
clean **"Added N to <player>"** summary showing every PlayStyle that went on. If any
couldn't be applied, it says so - nothing silently fails.

**📱 Works on your phone too.**
On a computer it's a wide two-pane workspace. On a phone it becomes a friendly
step-by-step flow - **① pick a player → ② choose PlayStyles → ③ apply** - with big,
thumb-friendly buttons.

**🛡️ Gentle and safe.**
It clicks the game's own buttons (it never fakes anything), and there's a **delay**
control so applies aren't fired all at once. Change your mind mid-run? Hit **Stop**.

**🎨 Looks the part.**
An "Emerald frosted glass" theme that sits nicely over the web app, with the game's
own PlayStyle icons throughout.

## Apply PlayStyles in 4 steps

1. **Pick a player** - search or scroll, then click/tap their row.
2. *(Optional)* **✨ Suggest** - choose a position + role for a recommended set, or
   just tick your own.
3. **Tick the PlayStyles** you want on the PlayStyle+ and PlayStyle tabs. The counter
   keeps you within the caps and greys out ones the player already has.
4. **Apply.** Watch the tiles tick off, then read the summary. Done - the player's
   card updates without reloading the page.

## Handy to know

- **Nothing to reload after an update.** Clicking the bookmark always loads the latest
  version and reuses your already-loaded club, so it's instant.
- **The caps are real.** A player can hold at most **3 PlayStyle+** and **8 Basic**
  PlayStyles - the tool won't let you exceed them.
- **Goalkeepers** get their own GK-only PlayStyles; those are hidden for outfielders
  automatically.
- **A failed apply is normal sometimes.** If a card can't take a particular PlayStyle
  (already has it, is maxed, or isn't an eligible card), that one is marked failed and
  the rest carry on.
- **Your settings stick.** The eligible-card list and the filter toggle are remembered
  between sessions.

Enjoy evolving your club. ⚽
